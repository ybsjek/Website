PreLaw Program

Our PreLaw program is designed for college students who are planning to go to law school and want real legal experience while they study.

What We Offer

Flexible Hours

We understand school comes first, so we offer flexible scheduling that works around classes and studying.

Real Courtroom Experience

Employees will get real courtroom experience and exposure to the legal process.

Hybrid Work Option

If you prefer, you can work in a hybrid work-from-home style so you can focus on your studies while still gaining experience.

Am I a Good Fit?

We are looking for people who are:

  • Currently in college or already college educated.
  • Honest and trustworthy.
  • Hardworking and reliable.
  • Interested in law, justice, and professional growth.
  • Strong communicators who can write and speak clearly.
  • Detail-oriented, organized, and able to follow instructions.
  • Professional, respectful, and eager to learn.
  • Able to balance responsibilities with school and work.
