What We Do

We handle all the paperwork

We prepare and file the necessary documents, manage deadlines, and ensure every procedural step is handled correctly so your judgment remains enforceable.

We work directly with the courts

We coordinate with court clerks and judicial staff, follow court procedures, and pursue enforcement actions on your behalf so you don’t have to navigate the system alone.

We find and secure assets

We locate assets the judgment debtor holds — including real estate, vehicles, intellectual property, business assets, and personal property — and identify options to seize or lien those assets to satisfy your judgment.
