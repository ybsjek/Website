Frequently Asked Questions

What is judgment enforcement?

Judgment enforcement is the process of using legal collection tools to try to recover money after a court judgment.

Can you help with small claims?

Yes. We help judgment creditors who won in small claims and need support collecting what is owed.

What is a judgment debtor?

A judgment debtor is the person or business that owes money under a court judgment.

Do you work in Washington, Idaho, and Oregon?

Yes. We serve judgment creditors across the Pacific Northwest.

How do I file in small claims?

Small claims filing usually starts with identifying the claim, completing the forms, and serving the other party properly.

What if the debtor does not pay?

If the debtor does not pay voluntarily, the next step may be judgment collection or enforcement.
