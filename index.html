How We Help You

Judgment Collection Assistance

If you won a court case and the other party refuses to pay, we take over the stressful part. We handle the paperwork, track the deadlines, and keep the pressure on the debtor until the account is settled.

Asset Hunting & Background Checks

People who owe money love to hide it. Our team specializes in digging deep to find hidden bank accounts, active employment records, secret business links, and property holdings that can be used to pay off your judgment.

Ways We Get You Paid

We use every legal tool available to tap into the debtor's income and property so your court order doesn't just sit on a shelf.

  • Wage Garnishment
  • Bank Account Garnishment
  • Property Liens
  • Real Estate Judgment Liens
  • Asset Seizures
  • Supplemental Proceedings
  • Debtor Asset Investigations
  • Collection Agency Coordination
