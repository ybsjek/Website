Who We Are

Northwest Judgment Enforcement was created with a simple purpose: to help judgment creditors pursue what they are owed in a way that is steady, effective, and grounded in fairness. We believe a judgment should mean something, and our work focuses on helping clients move from a court victory to actual recovery.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help clients pursue lawful collection with a thoughtful process, clear communication, and a strategy tailored to each case.

Our Approach

We start by understanding the judgment, identifying possible sources of recovery, and choosing the most practical enforcement path forward.

What Drives Us

We are guided by the idea that justice should not stop at the courtroom door. Our goal is to help clients bring their judgments to a meaningful result.

What the Process Looks Like

Step 1: Consultation

No two cases move the same way, which is why every client starts with a one-on-one consultation and a plan tailored to the details of the judgment.

Step 2: Locate Assets and Income

We identify where the debtor’s assets and income are held by reviewing county and state records and, when needed, using court proceedings to uncover more information.

Step 3: Place Liens

Once assets are identified, we place liens where available to create leverage and encourage payment.

Step 4: Finalize Payment

When the judgment is resolved, we help complete the process and record the satisfaction of judgment so the matter is fully closed.
