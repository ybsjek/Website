Won Your Court Case But Haven't Been Paid?

Winning in court is hard enough. Finding the money can be even harder. We help people and businesses find assets and enforce court judgments so you get paid.

What the Process Looks Like

Step 1: Consultation

No two cases move the same way, which is why every client starts with a one-on-one consultation and a plan tailored to the details of the judgment.

Step 2: Locate Assets and Income

We identify where the debtor’s assets and income are held by reviewing county and state records and, when needed, using court proceedings to uncover more information.

Step 3: Place Liens

Once assets are identified, we place liens where available to create leverage and encourage payment through the legal process.

Step 4: Finalize Payment

When the judgment is resolved, we help complete the process and record the satisfaction of judgment so the matter is fully closed.

How We Help

We work through the court system on your behalf, using the enforcement tools available to press a judgment forward and help bring a lawful result to completion.

Consultation and Strategy

We begin by reviewing your judgment, understanding the debtor, and building a practical plan based on your situation.

Court Representation

We work through the court process, handle the enforcement steps, and move the matter forward with a clear focus on results.

Asset Pressure

By using liens and other collection tools where appropriate, we create legal pressure that helps encourage payment.

How It Works

We offer three different arrangements so clients can choose the approach that fits their needs and timeline.

Standard

Under the Standard arrangement, the judgment is split 70/30.

Expedited

Under the Expedited arrangement, the judgment is split 50/50.

Instant

Under the Instant arrangement, you receive a cash payout in one to two weeks.
