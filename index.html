Won Your Court Case But Haven't Been Paid?

Winning in court is hard enough. Finding the money can be even harder. We help people and businesses find assets and enforce court judgments so you get paid.

Get Help Collecting Your Money

What the Process Looks Like

Step 1: Consultation

Personalized consultation. We build a specific plan based on your previous legal action and unique situation.

Step 2: Locate Assets and Income

Identify debtor’s assets and income by reviewing government records, subpoenas, and supplemental proceedings.

Step 3: Place Liens and Force Sale of Assets

Place liens on Debtor's assets and garnish wages. Eligible debtor's assets are seized and sold. leverage and encourage payment through the legal process.

Step 4: Finalize Payment

Secure payments are made to our client until the case is resolved.

How We Help

We work through the court system on your behalf, using the enforcement tools available to press a judgment forward and help bring a lawful result to completion.

Consultation and Strategy

We begin by reviewing your judgment, understanding the debtor, and building a practical plan based on your situation.

Court Representation

We work through the court process, handle the enforcement steps, and move the matter forward with a clear focus on results.

Asset Pressure

By using liens and other collection tools where appropriate, we create legal pressure that helps encourage payment.

How It Works

Judgment enforcement is not always a quick process and we are here to serve you for as long as it takes. However, some people seek faster resolution. To accommodate the needs of our customers, we have the following options.

Standard

70/30 split and collection is split each time payment comes in.

Expedited

Collections are split 50/50, but the customer is paid first.

Instant

You receive a cash payout in one to two weeks.
